Norwegian Boller




Description

Boller are a sweet and fluffy roll common as a snack all over Norway! They're easy to make and taste good with raisins or chocolate chips if you'd like to add some flavor!


Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Mix yeast, milk, flour, sugar, cardamom, egg, melted butter, and salt.
  2. Knead of floured surface until smooth.
  3. Leave in greased bowl for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 F.
  5. Divide dough into 10 pieces and roll into balls before placing on baking sheet. Let sit for 30 minutes.
  6. Bake in oven for 15 minutes and serve.
