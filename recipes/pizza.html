Pepperoni Pizza




Description

A simple and quick pizza recipe, utilizing premade pizza crust for busy folks without time to make their own.


Ingredients

  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded cheese of choice
  • Premade pizza crust
  • Sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F.
  2. Spread tomato sauce over surface of premade pizza crust.
  3. Cover with as much cheese as you'd like.
  4. Evenly place pepperonis on top of the cheese.
  5. Place on pan and bake for 15 minutes or until edges start to brown.
  6. Slice and serve.
